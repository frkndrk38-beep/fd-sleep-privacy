# Privacy Policy

FD Sleep Tracker respects your privacy.

The app may use:

- Notifications
- Local storage
- Internet connection
- Microphone access (for sleep sound analysis)
- Google AdMob advertising services

Sleep sound recordings are stored locally on the user's device and are used only for sleep analysis features.

Google AdMob and Google Play Services may collect anonymous usage information for analytics and advertising purposes.

FD Sleep Tracker does not sell personal information.
